About Us

Welcome to Telecall Laguna

We are an institution accredited by TESDA that offers Training Courses for Technical and Vocational Education (TVET) under Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

TESDA, an institution of higher learning, offers free study courses aimed at training trainees across various fields. By providing accessible vocational education and training, TESDA empowers individuals with the skills needed to succeed in today's competitive job market.
